The style that I use in this portfolio is'Isometric Illustration'. Isometric art is a drawing or illustration style that makes two-dimensional figures appear three dimensional. Isometric images can illustrate interiors, exteriors, objects, or logos with height, width, and depth to create the illusion of a 3D perspective.
